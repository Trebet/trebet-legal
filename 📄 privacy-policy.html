Privacy Policy

Trebet uses Firebase Authentication to allow users to create accounts using their email address.

Data collected

  • Email address
  • App usage data
  • Device information

Purpose of data usage

  • Account creation and authentication
  • App functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention

Data deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and personal data by emailing: trebetit@gmail.com

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
